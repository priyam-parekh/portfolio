    Why this UI?

    Basing it off the tagline "Building products people love", people loved the Macintosh, being a pioneer in great product design and UI. With that theme in mind, I designed my portfolio to resemble the Macintosh IIGS interface which is known for having advanced graphics capabilities at the time. Many of its design principles/style can still be found on the modern macOS, showing its long-lasting impact on user interfaces and experiences.
